Fenugreek Seeds Health Benefits

Fenugreek seeds called Methi seeds in India, are widely used in cooking and traditional remedies.

They contain fiber, iron, and beneficial compounds like saponins and antioxidants.

Helps Control Blood Sugar

Fenugreek seeds can improve insulin function.

Often used to help manage diabetes.

Improves Digestion

High fiber helps reduce constipation, gas, and indigestion.

Supports Weight Loss

Fiber in fenugreek makes you feel full for longer, reducing overeating.

Improves Heart Health

May help lower cholesterol levels and support heart health.

Good for Hair Growth

Fenugreek seeds are rich in protein and nicotinic acid, which may help reduce hair fall.

Improves Skin Health

Antioxidants help reduce acne, inflammation, and skin irritation.

Helps Increase Breast Milk

Traditionally used to help increase milk production in breastfeeding mothers.

Reduces Inflammation

Contains compounds that help reduce inflammation and joint pain.

How to Use Fenugreek Seeds

Soak 1 teaspoon fenugreek seeds overnight and drink the water in the morning.

Add to curries, pickles, and spice mixes.

Use fenugreek powder in herbal drinks.

Precautions

Consume in small amounts.

Pregnant women should consult a doctor before using large amounts.
