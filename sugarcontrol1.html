Ingredients for Sugarcontrol

1. Fenugreek seeds

2. Bitter Gourd

3. Jamun

4. Cinnamon

Process - 1 ==> Fenugreek Seeds

Step:1 Soak 1 teaspoon seeds overnight

Step:2 Drink the water in the morning (empty stomach)

Step:3 Chew soaked seeds

Process 2 ==> Bitter Gourd

Step:1 Drink fresh Bittergourd juice

Or cook and eat 2-3 times weekly

Process - 3 ==> Jamun

Step:1 Eat fresh jamun

Or take jamun seed powder 1/2 teaspoon with water

Process - 4 ==> Cinnamon

Step:1 Add 1/2 teasponn cinnamon powder to warm water or milk

Drink once daily
