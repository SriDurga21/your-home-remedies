Cloves Health Benefits

Cloves called Laung in India, are aromatic flower buds used in cooking and traditional medicine.

They contain a powerful compound called eugenol, which provides many health benefits.

Improves Digestion

Helps reduce gas, bloating, and indigestion.

Stimulates digestive enzymes for better digestion.

Relieves Tooth Pain

Clove oil has natural pain-relieving and antibacterial properties.

Often used to reduce toothache and gum pain.

Boosts Immunity

Rich in antioxidants that help the body fight infections.

Supports Respiratory Health

Helps relieve cough, cold, and throat irritation.

Clove tea can help clear mucus.

Helps Control Blood Sugar

May help regulate blood sugar levels.

Supports Liver Health

Eugenol in cloves may help protect the liver from damage.

Anti-Inflammatory Properties

Helps reduce inflammation and joint pain.

Good for Skin

Antibacterial properties may help reduce acne and skin infections.

How to Use Cloves

Add cloves while cooking biryani, curries, or soups.

Drink clove tea for cough relief.

Chew 1 clove after meals for digestion and fresh breath.

Precautions

Use in moderate amounts.

Excess clove or clove oil may cause mouth or stomach irritation.
