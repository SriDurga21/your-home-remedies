Health benefits of Coriander

Coriander seeds (called dhania seeds in India) are small but surprisingly powerful for health.

Improves Digestion

Coriander seeds help reduce bloating, gas, and indigestion.

They stimulate digestive enzymes, making your stomach work more efficiently.

Helps Control Blood Sugar

They are beneficial for people with diabetes.

Coriander seeds can help lower blood sugar levels by improving insulin activity.

Supports Heart Health

These seeds help reduce bad cholesterol (LDL) and increase good cholesterol (HDL),

lowering the risk of heart disease.

Good for Skin

Coriander seeds have antibacterial and antioxidant properties,

which can help with acne, rashes, and skin irritation.

Natural Detoxifier

They help flush out toxins from the body and support kidney function.

Helps with Blood Pressure

They have a mild diuretic effect, which helps reduce high blood pressure.

Boosts Immunity

Rich in antioxidants, coriander seeds help your body fight infections and inflammation.

Helps with Menstrual Issues

Coriander seed water is often used to relieve menstrual cramps and regulate cycles.

How to use Coriander

Soaked water: Soak 1 tsp seeds overnight, drink in the morning

Boiled water: Boil seeds, strain, and drink

Powder: Add to food or warm water

Precautions

Avoid large amounts during pregnancy

Too much can cause stomach upset or diarrhea

Stop before surgery (affects blood sugar)
