Fennel Health Benefits

Fennel seeds called Saunf in India, are commonly used after meals and in cooking.

They contain antioxidants, fiber, and essential oils that provide many health benefits.

Improves Digestion

Helps relieve gas, bloating, and indigestion.

Often eaten after meals to support digestion.

Freshens Breath

Natural antibacterial properties help reduce bad breath.

Supports Heart Health

Contains potassium and fiber, which help regulate blood pressure and cholesterol.

Helps Control Blood Sugar

May help regulate blood sugar levels.

Rich in Antioxidants

Protects the body from cell damage and inflammation.

Helps with Weight Management

Fiber helps you feel full longer, which may reduce overeating.

Supports Eye Health

Contains vitamin A and antioxidants that support healthy vision.

Good for Skin

Antioxidants help keep skin healthy and glowing.

How to Use Fennel Seeds

Chew ½-1 teaspoon fennel seeds after meals.

Drink fennel water or fennel tea.

Add to curries, salads, and spice mixes.

Precautions

Use in moderate amounts.

Excess consumption may cause allergic reactions in some people.
