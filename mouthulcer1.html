Ingredients for Mouthulcer

1.Salt water

2.Aloevera

3.Turmeric

Process - 1 ==> Salt Water Rinse

Step:1 Mix 1/2 teaspoon salt in warm water

Step:2 Rinse mouth 2-3 times daily

Process - 2 ==> Aloevera Gel

Step:1 Apply fresh aloe gel on ulcer

Step:2 Leave for 10-15 minutes

Process - 3 ==> Turmeric Paste

Step:1 Mix turmeric with few drops water

Step:2 Apply gently on ucler
