Ingredients for Cough

1.Honey

2.Ginger

3.Turmeric

Process - 1 ==> Honey

Step:1 Take 1 spoon of honey directly or mix with warm water

Step:2 You can add a few drops of lemon.

Take 2 - 3 times daily

Process - 2 ==> Ginger juice

Step:1 Crush fresh ginger

Step:2 Extract 1 teaspoon juice

Step:3 Mix with honey and take twice daily.

Process - 3 ==> Turmeric

Step:1 Add 1/2 spoon turmeric to warm milk

Step:2 Drink before bedtime.
