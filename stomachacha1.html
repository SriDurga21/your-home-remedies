Ingredients for Stomachacha

1. Ginger Tea

2. Cumin Water

3. Ajwain

Process - 1 ==> Ginger Tea

Step:1 Boil crushed ginger in 1 cup water

Step:2 Strain and drink warm

Step:3 Take 1-2 times daily

Process - 2 ==> Jeera Water

Step:1 Boil 1 teaspoon cumin seeds in water

Step:2 Cool and drink

Process - 3 ==> Ajwain

Step:1 Chew 1/2 teaspoon ajwain with warm water

Or boil in water and drink
