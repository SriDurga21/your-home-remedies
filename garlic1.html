Garlic Health Benefits

Garlic is widely used in cooking and traditional medicine.

It contains powerful compounds like allicin, vitamins, and antioxidants that support overall health.

Boosts Immunity

Garlic helps the body fight infections like cold and flu.

Its antibacterial and antiviral properties strengthen the immune system.

Supports Heart Health

May help lower blood pressure.

Helps reduce bad cholesterol (LDL) levels.

Improves blood circulation and reduces risk of heart disease.

Helps Control Blood Sugar

Garlic can improve insulin sensitivity, which may help manage blood sugar levels.

Rich in Antioxidants

Contains antioxidants that protect cells from damage caused by free radicals.

May slow aging and support brain health.

Improves Digestion

Stimulates digestive enzymes.

Helps reduce gas, bloating, and indigestion.

Anti-Inflammatory Properties

Garlic helps reduce inflammation in the body, which may help with joint pain and arthritis.

Detoxifies the Body

Helps the liver remove toxins and heavy metals from the body.

Good for Skin and Hair

Antibacterial properties may help reduce acne.

Nutrients in garlic can support hair growth.

Best Ways to Use Garlic

Eat 1-2 raw garlic cloves in the morning with warm water.

Add to curries, soups, or stir-fried vegetables.

Drink garlic tea for immunity.

Precautions

Avoid eating too much raw garlic (may cause stomach irritation).

People taking blood-thinning medicines should consult a doctor.
