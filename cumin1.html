Cumin Health Benefits

Cumin called Jeera in India, is a popular spice used in many dishes.

It is rich in iron, antioxidants, and essential oils that support overall health.

Improves Digestion

Stimulates digestive enzymes.

Helps reduce gas, bloating, and indigestion.

Boosts Immunity

Contains antioxidants that help protect the body from infections.

Helps Control Blood Sugar

May help regulate blood sugar levels and improve insulin function.

Supports Weight Loss

Cumin can help boost metabolism and reduce body fat.

Rich in Iron

Good source of iron, which helps prevent anemia and improves energy levels.

Improves Heart Health

May help reduce cholesterol levels and support healthy blood circulation.

Detoxifies the Body

Helps the liver remove toxins and supports overall body detoxification.

Good for Skin

Antioxidants help keep skin healthy and glowing.

How to Use Cumin

Drink jeera water (boil 1 teaspoon cumin seeds in water).

Add to curries, rice, and soups.

Use roasted cumin powder in salads and buttermilk.

Precautions

Consume in moderate amounts.

Excess cumin may cause stomach irritation.
