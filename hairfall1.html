Ingredients for hairfall

1.Fenugreek seeds

2.Onion

3.Coconut Oil

Process - 1 ==> Fenugreek seeds

Step:1 Soak seeds overnight

Step:2 Grind into paste

Step:3 Apply on scalp for 30 minutes

Step:4 Wash off

Process - 2 ==> Onion

Step:1 Extract onion juice

Step:2 Apply to scalp

Step:3 Leave 20 - 30 minutes

Step:4 Wash with mild shampoo

Process - 3 ==> Coconut Oil

Step:1 Warm coconut oil slightly

Step:2 Massage gently on scalp for 10 - 15 minutes

Step:3 Leave for 1 hour (or overnight)

Step:4 Wash with mild shampoo
