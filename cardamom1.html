Cardamom Health Benefits

Cardamom also called Elaichi, is a fragrant spice widely used in Indian cooking, tea, and traditional medicine.

It contains antioxidants, essential oils, and minerals that support good health.

Improves Digestion

Helps relieve gas, bloating, and indigestion.

Stimulates digestive enzymes and improves gut health.

Freshens Breath

Natural antibacterial properties help fight bad breath.

Chewing cardamom seeds can keep the mouth fresh.

Supports Heart Health

May help lower blood pressure.

Improves blood circulation and supports heart function.

Rich in Antioxidants

Protects cells from damage caused by free radicals.

Helps reduce inflammation in the body.

Helps Detox the Body

Supports kidney function and helps remove toxins from the body.

Improves Respiratory Health

Helps relieve cough, asthma, and breathing problems.

Cardamom tea can help clear mucus.

Helps Control Blood Sugar

Some studies suggest cardamom may help regulate blood sugar levels.

Good for Skin

Antioxidants help improve skin health and may reduce skin infections.

How to Use Cardamom

Add to tea (elaichi chai).

Use in desserts, curries, and rice dishes.

Chew 1-2 pods after meals for digestion and fresh breath.

Precautions

Use in moderate amounts.

Excess consumption may cause stomach discomfort.
