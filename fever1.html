Ingredients for Fever

1. Tulsi Tea

2. Ginger Tea

3. Coriander

4. Cold compress

Process - 1 ==> Tulsi Tea

Step:1 Boil 5-7 tulsi leaves in 1 cup water

Step:2 Boil for 5-10 minutes

Step:3 Drink warm 2 times daily

Process - 2 ==> Ginger Tea

Step:1 Boil crushed ginger in water

Step:2 Strain and add honey

Drink warm

Process - 3 ==> Coriander

Step:1 Boil 1 spoon coriander seeds in water

Step:2 Strain and drink warm

Process - 4 ==> Cold compress

Step:1 Dip cloth in cool water

Step:2 Place on forehead

Step:3 Repeat when it becomes warm
