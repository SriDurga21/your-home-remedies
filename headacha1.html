Ingredients for Headacha

1.Ginger

2.Cold or Warm Compress

3.Proper Rest & Hydration

4.Clove paste

Process - 1 ==> Ginger Tea

Step:1 Boil crushed ginger in 1 cup water

Step:2 Strain and drink warm

Step:3 Take 1-2 times daily

Process - 2 ==> Cold or Warm Compress

Cold compress -> good for migraine

Warm compress -> good for stree headache

Step:1 Place cloth with cold/warm water on forehead or neck for 10-15 minutes

Process - 3 ==> Proper Rest & Hydration

Step:1 Drink enough water

Step:2 Sleep 7-8 hours

Step:3 Avoid too much mobile screen

Process - 4 ==> Clove paste

Step:1 Crush 3-4 cloves

Step:2 Mix with little water or coconut oil

Step:3 Apply paste on forehead
